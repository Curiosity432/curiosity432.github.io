Fortnite was a game released at the end of July 2017, its original name was Fortnite: Save the World with payment for early access with plans to be free-to-play what would be Fortnite: Battle Royale. Its first announcement was in 2012 .

Fortnite: Save the World#

One day, 98% of Earth’s human population suddenly disappeared, and the remaining humans found the skies covered with dense clouds, creating chaotic storms that dropped husks: zombie-like humanoid creatures that attacked the living. Survivors found a way to build “storm shields,” a field that cleared the storm clouds immediately above and reduced husk attacks, and used them to establish survivor bases around the world. The player is the commander of one of these bases, tasked with breaking out of the storm shield to find resources, survivors, and other allies to help them expand their storm shield and find a way to return Earth to its normal state.

Fortnite is set on contemporary Earth, where the sudden onset of a global storm causes 98% of the world’s population to disappear, and zombie-like creatures rise up to attack the rest. Considered by Epic as a cross between Minecraft and Left 4 Dead, in Fortnite up to four players cooperate in various missions on different maps to collect resources, build fortifications around defensive objectives that should help fight the storm and protect survivors, and build weapons and traps to engage in combat with waves of these creatures intent on destroying the objectives. Players earn rewards through these missions to upgrade their hero characters, their support teams, and their arsenal of weapon and trap schemes in order to tackle more difficult missions.

For some reason one day, 98% of the humans on Earth suddenly disappeared, and the remaining population found that the skies were covered by a strange purple storm that has the property of creating monsters called husks, humanoid zombie-like creatures that attacked living people. The survivors found ways to build Storm Shields, a field that cleared storm clouds from above, and reduced the attacks of the husks, and used them to establish survivor bases around the world. The player is a commander of one of these bases, tasked with going outside the storm shield to find resources, survivors and other allies to help expand their storm shield and find a way to return Earth to its normal state.

In September 2017, Fortnite: Battle Royale was released for free and for all platforms. This mode surpassed 10 million players in the first two weeks of its release

Fortnite: Battle Royale#

Fortnite Battle Royale is a free-to-play battle royale video game developed and published by Epic Games. It is a companion game to Fortnite: Save the World, a cooperative survival game with building elements. It was initially released in early access on September 26, 2017, for Microsoft Windows, macOS, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One, followed by ports for iOS, Android, and Nintendo Switch the following year. Epic removed the early access label for the game on June 29, 2020. PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S versions were released as launch titles in late 2020.

The concept of the game is similar to previous games in the genre: 100 players parachute onto an island and seek out equipment to defend themselves against other players. Players can fight alone (Solo), with one other player (Duos), three players (Trios), or with a group of four (Squads). As the game progresses, the playable area inside the island gradually narrows, giving players less and less room to work with; outside this safe zone is “the Storm,” which inflicts damage on those trapped inside it, and the amount of damage increases as the Storm itself does. The last player or team alive wins the game. The main distinction from other games in the genre is the game’s construction elements, which allow players to build walls, obstacles and other structures with collected resources to provide cover from incoming gunfire or give a strategic view advantage. Battle Royale uses a seasonal approach with battle passes to introduce new character customization content into the game, as well as limited-time events, some of which correspond with changes to the game map. Since its initial release, additional game modes have been introduced, such as “Battle Lab” and “Party Royale.”

Stark Industries is a company from Earth: 616 in Fortnite: Battle Royale. They create robots, weapons and much more. It is Tony Stark’s company that develops Iron Man suits, Stark robots that patrol Quinjet patrol sites, rift beacons to take to upstate New York, and weaponry to use against Galactus.
