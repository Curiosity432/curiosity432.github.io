I am going to write about this topic after almost two years since I started. To give a summary of how I thought before and how I think now, how things have evolved and put some perspective to the matter.

Covid-19 started at the official level in December 2019 in Wuhan, China, in a market area and through the contagion of a batsman it reached humans.

Once China started to spread globally, slowly through different countries, a monumental hype started to be created about the issue.

Many newspapers started talking about it, on the radio, TV, everywhere. It was already a topic of discussion and among friends it was also talked about. I remember in January 2020 how people were already starting to talk about these issues and to laugh it off as “Oh, we are all going to die”.

January passed and February arrived, the month of Carnival in Tenerife with the earliest carnivals in history. There was the strongest haze in history and the carnivals were celebrated, some wore masks and others did not to protect themselves from the dust in supension that did not allow to see beyond 500 meters.

I remember going in disguise as Biohazard and hearing people comment, “This is either because of the haze or because of the coronavirus.”
