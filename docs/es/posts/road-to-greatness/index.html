Camino hacia la grandeza

abril 1, 2024 · 1 min · Curiosity432

The distractions that envelop us are on the rise, Like lab mice, we feel trapped in a maze of incessant noise, A deluge of publications flood our senses, Creating illusions, veiling truths, stifling our defenses.

But amidst this chaos, do not let others dictate your course, For humans are not mere automatons, programmed to endorse. We are not clones, shackled by limitations, We are beings imbued with extraordinary inclinations.

We transcend the confines of time and space, Igniting the sparks of creativity, we embrace, In the midst of uncertainty, we stand tall, Guiding others with hope, breaking barriers, overcoming all.

Do not let past shadows or present hurdles deter, For within you lies a vast reservoir of power to confer. You are the architect of your fate, the master of your tale, With each step forward, let your potential prevail.

Embrace the courage within, defy the naysayers’ taunt, For the human spirit knows no bounds, it will jaunt. Stand firm, awaken to your celestial call, For you hold the keys to unlock the universe’s thrall.

In your hands, lies the alchemy to transform dreams into sight, To etch your destiny in stars, in letters of light.
